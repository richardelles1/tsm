## 7. Payables engine intent

Operational model:

1. **Release is created**
2. System **automatically creates a payable record**
3. Admin sees payables in a **batch review screen**
4. Admin triggers payout batch (Stripe / bank)
5. Payables marked **paid**

So:

* Releases = money unlocked
* Payables = money actually sent

Admin approves batches, not individual payouts.
